Tesla's Revolutionary Inventions

Radio Technology

Tesla made groundbreaking contributions to the development of radio technology, including wireless transmission.

Tesla Coil

The Tesla Coil, invented in 1891, was capable of producing high-voltage, low-current, high-frequency alternating-current electricity.

AC Power System

Tesla's alternating current (AC) power system revolutionized the way electricity is distributed and used worldwide.
